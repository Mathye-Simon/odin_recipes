Kota

The Kota king

It's is very simple to make, yet so majestic! It comes with a lot of variations. We'll be making the most commonly made in South Africa.

In this recipe oil will be used for most of the ingredients because they have to be fried. Some sauces of your choice or the ones I reccomend and a bit of vegetables to perfect the equation.

Ingredients

  • Quarter loaf of bread
  • Slice of polony
  • Lettuce
  • Sliced cuccumber
  • Potatoes
  • Seasoning (salt and aromat)
  • Complimentary sauces
  • Cooking oil
