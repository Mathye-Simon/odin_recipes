Amagwinya

Crispy South African Amagwinya

The perfect amagwinya according to an expert(me), is the one that is crispy on the outside and soft in the inside.

It is subtly sweet and salty with balance like no other, a bite sized snack for anytime of the day preferebly breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 600 g All-Purpose Flour
  • 1 Tablespoon cooking Oil
  • 10 g Instant Yeast
  • 3 Tablespoons Brown Sugar
  • 3 g Salt
  • 350 ml Lukewarm Water
  • Cooking Oil(for frying)

Steps

  1. In a large mixing bowl, sieve in flour, then add yeast, sugar and, mix well.
  2. Add 1 tablespoon of cooking oil and water. Then start mixing with your hands. Mix for 11 to 12 minutes until the dough is formed (but still sticky as this is a sticky dough).
  3. Close the mixing bowl and leave the dough for about 30 minutes until it rises
  4. Meanwhile heat up the oil, until the oil is hot enough.
  5. After the dough has risen. Scoup a piece of the dough out of the container with you hands then form a small ball.
  6. Throw/put that dough ball inside the hot cooking oil same time make other balls and cook them at the same time depending on the capacity of your saucepan or deep-fryer
  7. Fry all sides unti they are crispy brown. And you got yourself crispy Amagwinya
